Join us in making Richmond a better place for everyone.

Richmond Action Alliance is a brand new grassroots non-profit aimed at supporting the local community with voter registration, racial justice, education, and bridging gaps within the community and local government. We have grown under the love and support of the community and we are thrilled to be able to put all that love and support right back into the community that we call home.

Keep an eye on our website and social media pages for up-to-date information on upcoming events hosted by us and other organizations within the community!
